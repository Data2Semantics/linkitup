Linkitup taps into your favourite research data repository, and enriches your datasets with links to vocabularies, registries and the Linked Data cloud.

Publish the metadata of your dataset as Linked Data. Linkitup allows you to publish the metadata you generated as Figshare.com dataset as well as directly to the Linked Data Cloud.

Linkitup is developed as part of the COMMIT/ project Data2Semantics, which is committed to bringing Linked Science to the masses.
